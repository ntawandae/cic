My story

Hie my name is Cidella, college student, self-taught hairstylist and content creator, living in China after moving from my homeland of China.

After coming to China about two years ago i quickly discovered that they are not so many people of African descendacy so i quickly realised that noone will do your hair for you. I stated doing my friends hair and quickly discovered that most people liked that now i do maybe three to five people,s hair a week. Next time when you are in Qingdao make sure to book your hair appointment with me.

Before i come to China i didn't know a lot about living in China. I know that they are so many stories you can read online but its not the same as someone really telling you the actually story. Knowing this made me want to create a youtube channel where i share with my subscribers college stories, how life is for foreigners in China,study tips and a lot more revolving around my life in China
